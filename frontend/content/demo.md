## A sandbox you can actually use

The demo is a **fully working copy of ICCP** running against a shared demo
organisation — no account, no login, nothing to install.

<p><a class="btn btn-main" href="https://compliance-demo.isolatedcommand.com/dashboard/">Open the demo →</a></p>

What's inside:

- **ISO 27001 and Cyber Trust Mark adopted**, with a realistic half-assessed posture
- **Six controls** cross-mapped to ISO, SOC 2, CIS v8 and Cyber Trust Mark requirements
- **Evidence in every lifecycle state** — approved, pending review, and expired
- **A live risk register** from critical to accepted
- **Approved policies** and an **internal audit in fieldwork** with open findings

Everything is editable — assess requirements, add controls, register risks,
record findings. It is a shared sandbox: other visitors see your changes, and
the whole organisation **resets every night**.

When you want your own tenant, use the real platform at
[compliance.isolatedcommand.com](https://compliance.isolatedcommand.com/) —
sign-in is protected by Cloudflare Access.
